Integrated Supplement & Neuromodulation Plan

Purpose

This integrated plan coordinates supplements, non-drug neuromodulation, and an adjunct cannabinoid option to reduce central neuropathic pain, improve sleep and autonomic stability, and support neuro-regeneration following a right-sided brainstem stroke.

Adjunct Cannabinoid Option

For severe neuropathic flare where conventional neuropathic agents are not tolerated, a balanced THC:CBD (1:1) vapourised dose under 0.25 ml nightly may be trialled under clinical supervision for pain, sleep, and mood stabilisation.
