Protocol

  • Magnesium
    • Glycinate or L-Threonate forms, working toward 200-300mg elemental in lightweight format (bisket late format) e.g. 2 x 200mg if in bisketlate format and work toward escalating towards 300-400mg every 3-7 days to tolerance and tolerated symptom improvement
  • Mitochondrial support & anti-nociceptive support next: Break a few days after magnesium if necessary
    • ALA 300mg NaR-lipoic acid (or R-Lipoic Acid) in particular
    • Acetyl-L-Carnitine (A-L-Carnitine) 500mg regularly
  • Omega-3’s
    • Omega EPA with some high quality DHA (ethical fish sources): Doses depend on budget. Range is 1.5-3g EPA DHA based but higher end recommended if possible. EPA would be more than DHA here.
  • Anti-inflammatory supplements
    • NAC 600mg twice daily
    • Curcumin – spend more on this one, twice daily if possible
  • Trigeminal – Headache/migraine – occipital nerve normalisation & balance/therapies
    • Gentle physiotherapy style massage e.g. small amount of friction massage where neck meets head going across the line briefly for 60 seconds; gentle, regular massages supportive (including cranial not deep stimulation massage type); massage is good for soothing the occipital nerves – steady regular without massive changes for conditions like migraine (just gentle & massage-looking-for-pressure changes that are soothing); I suspect cranial gentle is helpful
    • Occipital TENS (gently)
    • Infrared or cold-laser
  • Breathing and HRV daily and freedom to trial yoga-stretches basic physiology not pushing range of motion after integrative therapy plan in place

Intergrated Recovery Plan Daily Plan · Tick-off Checklist.

Breath, Coherence & Movement

Breath, coherence & movement as non drug neuromodulation (plus accupuncture if possible), circulation support while improving neurological function initially

  • HRV coherence breathing is something that can be powerful in real-time
  • You can build toward 10 minutes twice a day
  • You can also incorporate intermittent breaks to get a reset
  • It is powerful for immediate effect but often more especially developmentally over 2-6 weeks
  • You can also practice that 5-second breathing window for focus

Initial Anti-inflammatory & Pain-management Supplements

  • Magnesium (glycinate or L-Threonate), turmeric (spent a little more on this one), NAC – this group are multi-purpose
  • Magnesium considered to regulate a lot and calm signals as well as being structural within neuron etc.
  • Curcumin forms are often highly effective
  • NAC is general purpose, also can be useful in sleep
  • Three together powerful combination

Neuromodulation

  • Cannabinoids can be powerful here even at a small dose and balanced.

Sleep

  • Sleep structure, difficult to maintain an environment that is predictable and comfortable – dark, cool, fairly structured, aim for 6.5 hours and then gradually work toward 8 but do not force if for any reason it is detrimental

Supplement Details & Quantities

  • Magnesium glycinate preferred and sometimes magnesium L-threonate
  • Fish oil: EPA based, with some DHA as well. If getting algae-oil derived or fish-sourced then ok
  • NAC 600mg twice daily typically
  • ALA R-lipoic acid preferred (NaR-LA better if possible) 300mg doses – these two are powerful but may create side effects so careful and balance with a physician friendly approach; I want to make sure you’re able to work on using these safely if you use them
  • Acetyl-L-Carnitine 500mg regularly
  • Curcumin dosage twice daily as discussed
