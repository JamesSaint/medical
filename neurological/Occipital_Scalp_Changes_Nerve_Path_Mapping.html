Occipital Nerve Path Mapping

Nerve path mapping reference for clinical correlation.

Nerve key
  • Greater occipital nerve (C2): posterior scalp up to the vertex.
  • Lesser occipital nerve (C2–C3): lateral occiput and area behind the ear.
  • Third occipital nerve (C3): lower occiput and upper nape.

Each image caption indicates the most relevant nerve territory for visible hair or scalp changes.
