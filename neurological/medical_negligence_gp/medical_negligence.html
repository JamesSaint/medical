Chronology of GP Practice – Dr Hannah Morgan, Emsworth Medical

For review by legal counsel or medical tribunal

Background

Between 2021 and 2024, under the care of Dr Hannah Morgan at Emsworth Medical (Emsworth Surgery, North Street, Emsworth), I reported episodic onset of left‐sided scalp/occipital pain, facial numbness, visual disturbance, and sensory changes in the jaw/collarbone region, beginning within 24 hours of my AstraZeneca vaccine in 2021. Despite repeated presentations, no urgent neurological referral or imaging was initiated by the practice.

Chronology of Missed Escalations

  1. 2021 (post-vaccine onset): Initial complaints of occipital burning pain and facial numbness were managed conservatively (analgesics, reassurance) without referral or further investigation.
  2. 2022–2024: Recurrent episodic neurological symptoms recorded in GP notes (pain, sensory disturbance, visual blurring), with no escalation to neurology, no request for MRI, and no consideration of vaccine-related pathology.
  3. Early 2025: Symptoms worsened; left hemisensory neuropathic features became more consistent and functionally impairing. Still no GP referral to neurology.
  4. April 2025: I obtained a private referral via Doctor Care Anywhere (bypassing primary care), which led to prompt neurology review, imaging, and diagnostic work-up revealing a cerebellar infarct and suspect myelitis.

Analysis of Breach of Duty

  • The practice under Dr Morgan failed to respect **red‐flag neurology symptoms** (focal sensory loss, visual symptoms, unilateral pain distribution) by not initiating timely imaging or specialist referral.
  • No documented consideration of **post-vaccine neurological injury**, despite the temporal onset and evolving symptom pattern consistent with known rare vaccine complications.
  • The delay in diagnosis and appropriate investigation materially worsened neurological outcome, by prolonging untreated neuropathic injury and preventing earlier intervention.

Impact of the Breach

As a result of the delayed referral and mismanagement by Dr Morgan and Emsworth Medical, I endured prolonged neuropathic pain, significant functional impairment, and psychological distress. I was compelled to seek private diagnostic pathways to secure proper neurological assessment.
