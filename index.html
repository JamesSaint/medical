Medical Home – James Saint

Patient Statement of Record Stewardship

I maintain this personal archive to ensure that my medical history, diagnostic findings, and correspondence are preserved in full and in context. It is not a challenge to clinical authority but a commitment to clarity and accountability.

I have experienced how fragmented communication between providers can distort or erase parts of a patient’s story. This record brings those fragments together so that every clinician can see the same timeline, evidence, and reasoning.

My intention is collaboration: to work with my medical team on the basis of complete information, informed consent, and mutual respect. This archive exists so that the truth of my health remains intact, whatever changes occur in systems, policies, or personnel.

Legal and Ethical Framework

This archive is maintained in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. The patient, as data subject, retains full ownership and access rights to personal medical data. All third-party disclosures or sharing of these materials require explicit informed consent.

Under the Access to Health Records Act (1990), this archive also preserves clinical records from historical providers where applicable, ensuring full traceability and patient autonomy.
